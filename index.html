Who I Am

A Guide Born from
Real Adventures

I'm Mudassir — a travel guide, trip planner, and photographer based in Bengaluru. I don't just visit places; I curate experiences. From organising college trips to Wonderla, Goa, and Kolar, to leading groups of students across South India, I've spent years turning travel chaos into smooth, memorable journeys.

With 4 years as Class Representative, 3 years running college festivals, 2 years in corporate, and an ongoing MBA in Management — I bring structure, empathy, and enthusiasm to every trip I plan. When I'm not guiding, I'm behind a camera, documenting the world one frame at a time.
